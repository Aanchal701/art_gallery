Arjun Mehta
Hails From: Delhi

Arjun Mehta is a well-known contemporary artist who skillfully blends urban and rural landscapes in his surreal paintings. His work evokes a sense of dreamlike serenity, exploring the contrast between natural and man-made environments with extraordinary creativity. Through his art, he invites viewers to perceive the world through a different lens, challenging the boundaries of reality.
Priya Nair
Hails From: Kerala

Priya Nair is a gifted artist known for her expertise in traditional mural art. Her work revives ancient Indian painting techniques, merging them with contemporary concepts to create dynamic pieces that reflect the rich cultural history of India. Her vivid, detailed murals often tell stories of heritage, mythology, and nature, drawing attention to the beauty of traditional art forms in a modern context.
Ravi Deshpande
Hails From: Mumbai

Ravi Deshpande is a versatile multimedia artist, whose innovative approach spans a range of media including sculpture, digital art, and installation pieces. His artwork explores the complexities of modern society, often reflecting on themes of identity, politics, and the human condition. Through his installations, he creates immersive experiences that challenge conventional ideas and encourage social dialogue.
Meera Kapoor
Hails From: Kolkata

Meera Kapoor is a renowned photographer whose work captures the essence of India's diverse cultures through her poignant black-and-white portraits. With a deep focus on the human condition, her photographs convey stories of individuals from various walks of life, preserving moments that evoke emotion and reflection. Meera's evocative imagery offers viewers an intimate window into the soul of India.
Anjali Sharma
Hails From: Jaipur

Anjali Sharma is celebrated for her expertise in intricate miniature paintings, a form of traditional Indian art that she beautifully modernizes. Drawing inspiration from Rajasthan's cultural heritage, Anjali's delicate artworks showcase vibrant colors and exquisite detail, often depicting historical and cultural narratives. Her fusion of classic techniques with contemporary sensibilities has made her a significant voice in the world of Indian art.
